Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our gift card sweepstakes

Who is eligible to enter the sweepstakes?

You must be at least 18 years old and a legal resident of the United States to enter our sweepstakes. Employees of GiftCardSweeps.com and their immediate family members are not eligible to participate.

Is there any cost to enter?

No, there is absolutely no cost to enter our sweepstakes. We offer free entry methods as required by law. You will never be asked for credit card information to participate.

How often are winners selected?

Winners are selected daily through random drawings. The number of winners depends on the specific promotion and available prizes. All winners are notified via email within 24 hours of selection.

How will I receive my gift card if I win?

All gift cards are delivered digitally to the email address you provided during registration. Most winners receive their gift card codes within 24-48 hours of being notified. Physical gift cards are shipped to the address provided if required.

Can I enter multiple times?

Yes, you can enter each sweepstakes once per day. Some promotions may allow additional entries through bonus methods like referring friends or completing specific actions.

Where can I view the official rules?

The complete official rules for each sweepstakes can be found on the specific offer page. You can also access our general terms and conditions through the link in the website footer.
